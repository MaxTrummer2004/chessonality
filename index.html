Last updated: April 2026

1. Controller

2. Local-first storage

Chessonality is a local-first web app. Your display name, game history, analysis results, personality scores and theme preference are stored exclusively in your browser (IndexedDB and localStorage) on your own device. We do not operate a user database and do not transmit this data to our own servers.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR (performance of the service you requested) and § 165 (3) TKG 2021 (technically necessary local storage).

3. Game import from Lichess and Chess.com

When you enter a Lichess or Chess.com username, your browser fetches your public games directly from the respective platform's public API. We do not handle credentials, and the request is made from your device, not from our servers.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR.

4. AI analysis (Anthropic Claude API)

To generate move explanations and coaching insights, chess positions (FEN strings), move lists and engine evaluations are transmitted to Anthropic PBC (548 Market St., PMB 90375, San Francisco, CA 94104, USA), the operator of the Claude API. We do not transmit your name or other directly identifying information. Anthropic acts as an independent recipient of the data; details are governed by Anthropic's privacy policy.

The transfer to the USA takes place on the basis of the EU–US Data Privacy Framework, in which Anthropic participates, as well as on the basis of EU Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR (provision of the analysis service you requested).

5. Chess engine evaluation

If you choose the Lichess cloud engine, the position to be analyzed is sent to the public Lichess analysis API. If you use the local engine, no data leaves your browser.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR.

6. Hosting and server logs

The static files of this website are delivered via a hosting provider. Like every web server, the hosting provider may temporarily process technical access data (IP address, user agent, time of request, requested URL) in server logs for the purpose of operating and securing the service.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR (legitimate interest in operating a stable and secure website).

7. Cookies and tracking

Chessonality does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, web analytics, fingerprinting or any third-party tracking. The only items stored locally are your theme preference and your local game data, both of which are technically necessary to provide the requested functionality and do not require consent under § 165 TKG 2021.

8. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

access (Art. 15), rectification (Art. 16), erasure (Art. 17), restriction of processing (Art. 18), data portability (Art. 20) and objection (Art. 21). Because all your personal data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise erasure and portability yourself at any time via Settings → Export Data and Settings → Delete Account, or by clearing your browser's site data.

9. Right to lodge a complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority. The competent authority in Austria is the Austrian Data Protection Authority (Datenschutzbehörde), Barichgasse 40–42, 1030 Vienna, dsb.gv.at.

10. Retention period

We do not retain any personal data on our servers. Locally stored data on your device persists until you delete it yourself. Server log files of the hosting provider are typically deleted after a few days.
